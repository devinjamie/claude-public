ASSETS FOLDER — DEVIN GRUBER PORTFOLIO
========================================

Drop your files here and update the HTML accordingly:

  logo.png         → Your logo graphic (already referenced in index.html)
  headshot.jpg     → Your photo for the About section
                     Then in index.html, replace the <img src="https://..."> in the
                     about section with: <img src="assets/headshot.jpg" alt="Devin Gruber">

PHOTOS
  photo-1.jpg through photo-8.jpg  (or any names you prefer)
  In the Photos section, replace each src="https://images.unsplash.com/..."
  with: src="assets/photo-1.jpg" etc.

  Recommended sizes: 1200px on the long edge, ~300-500KB each (JPG, quality 80)

VIDEOS
  Your videos are embedded via YouTube/Vimeo.
  See the comments in the Video section of index.html — 
  uncomment the <iframe> blocks and replace VIDEO_ID with your actual video IDs.
